        ===========================================
                        Serial I/O
        ===========================================
Serial communication is the simplest possible way to interface your microcontroller
with your desktop or laptop computer.

So, this begs the question.....how do computers or integrated circuit components
actually talk to one another?
Well one side sends a signal by outputting high and low voltage pulses on a wire that
connects it to the other device. The other side, in input mode, listens to the voltages
on the wire.
        ===========================================
                    Serial Communication
        ===========================================
The rules for encoding data into voltage pulses and decoding the voltage pulses back into
data is called a serial protocol. We can demonstrate this feature using the universal 
asynchronous receive and transmit(UART) signal.
